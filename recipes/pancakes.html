Tacos pancakes with bananas and Nutella

Some more Mexican-inspired pancakes for this dessert.

Ingredients:

  • 2 well-ripened bananas
  • 60 ml vegetable oil
  • 2 whole eggs
  • 30 g caster sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 160 ml milk with 3.5% fat
  • 150 g white flour
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 8 g baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon wine vinegar
  • 200-250 g Nutella

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, mix the flour with the baking powder.

  2. In a bowl put the eggs, sliced ​​bananas, milk, sugar, salt powder, vanilla extract and with a blender we will mix the composition until smooth.

  3. Add the flour mixed with the baking powder, and with the help of a fork we incorporate it as well, stirring vigorously.

  4. Add the wine vinegar and mix again.

  5. On a hot pan, put a few drops of oil and pour from the composition round shapes with the diameter we want. I made them with a diameter of 8-9 cm and 16 pieces came out.

  6. Bake on medium heat on both sides until nicely browned. Let them cool and grease them with Nutella, only half of them and then fold them.

  7. A hearty and delicious breakfast, but it goes just as well as dessert. Good job and good appetite!

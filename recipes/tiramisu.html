Tiramisu

Tiramisu, the classic recipe, or let's say the original recipe, meaning the recipe with raw eggs and alcohol in the composition,
is an Italian dessert that has become popular all over the world. Over time, a variety of tiramisu has appeared, with raw eggs,
cooked eggs, no eggs, whipped cream, fruit, fruit syrup instead of coffee, but originally the tiramisu recipe was like this. with raw egg,
coffee, mascarpone, espresso coffee and alcohol.Tiramisu is a cake that is very easy to taste and very tasty,
provided we use quality ingredients, the most important being mascarpone, coffee and alcohol, ingredients that give the final taste.

Ingredients

  • 500g mascarpone
  • 4 eggs
  • 150g powdered sugar
  • 300g biscuits
  • 300ml coffee
  • 2 spoons of Amaretto
  • 2 spoons cocoa

Steps

  1. Since raw eggs are used in this recipe, you must be very careful and use fresh eggs.
    Separate the yolks from the egg whites and mix the yolks with the sugar until the sugar is completely melted and the composition becomes creamy.
    If you have a problem with raw eggs you can beat the eggs on a steam bath.

  2. Add the mascarpone and mix with a spatula until incorporated into the egg mixture. Also now we add Amaretto liqueur in the composition or Marsala wine, but if the children will eat or you know that you are going to get behind the wheel, you can give up this stage. If you don't have Amaretto or Marsala you can deviate from the classic recipe and you can even use whiskey cream, coffee liqueur, or whatever you think would suit.

  3. We mix the egg whites until they become hard foam and with the help of a spatula we incorporate them, with circular movements.

  4. We prepare the coffee and set it aside to cool. I used espresso, which I sweetened very little. We quickly dip the biscuits in the coffee, if we keep them longer they will soak in the coffee and we will not get the perfect dessert.

  5. Place the coffee syrup biscuits on the bottom of a bowl.

  6. Add a layer of cream and then a new row of biscuits.

  7. We put the last layer of cream, level it and on top, with the help of a sieve, we sprinkle cocoa. For a perfect taste it is important to use a quality cocoa. Refrigerate overnight, then we can cut it into cubes, with a sharp knife, which we will wipe before each slice.

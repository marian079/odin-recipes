Pizza



A large disc of dough, covered with tomato paste, then either only grated cheese or pieces of mozzarella cheese,
or other toppings like chopped vegetables, sausages, salami, etc. and cheese, which is then baked together in
a very hot oven, then cut into slices so that one can eat it conveniently by taking one wedge-shaped slice at a time.

Ingredients:

For dough:
  • 400g flour
  • 25g yeast
  • 2 tablespoons sweet milk
  • 75 ml oil
  • 2 tablespoons yogurt
  • 50 ml mineral water
For topping:
  • 300g cheese
  • 200g olives
  • 2 peppers
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 2-3 tomatoes
  • one red onion
  • 2 parsley threads
  • 250g spicy and dry salami
  • spicy tomatoes sauce
  • savory

Steps:

  1. Sift the flour into a bowl

  2. Add the quenched yeast in 2 tablespoons of sweet and lukewarm milk, salt, oil, mineral water and yogurt

  3. Knead well and get an elastic dough, then let it rise next to a heat source, covered with a kitchen towel

  4. Separately, prepare the ingredients for the pizza, grate the cheese, cut the onions, tomatoes, olives and salami

  5. After the dough has risen, divide it into four pieces and roll out each piece with a rolling pin

  6. Sprinkle with cheese, pepper, salt, onion, salami, thyme, olives, pepper and tomato slices and put in the oven

  7. After baking, serve with spicy or sweet tomato sauce
